About Us

Waves for Change is a youth-led environmental initiative dedicated to tackling plastic pollution and promoting sustainability through community-driven action. Founded in June 2024, our project began with a simple yet powerful idea: transforming discarded plastic bottles into plant pots and using them to create positive change. What started as a series of small beach cleanups quickly grew into a movement, bringing together passionate volunteers to restore our coastlines while repurposing waste into something meaningful..

Our Mission

At Waves for Change, we strive to create a cleaner, greener world by merging environmental conservation with social impact. Through regular beach cleanups, creative upcycling, and community engagement, we aim to reduce plastic waste, raise awareness, and support charitable causes.
